Data Deletion Instructions

Your privacy is important to us. If you wish to delete your data associated with the Travel Companion App, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Request Data Deletion

  1. Log in to your Facebook account.
  2. Go to your Facebook App Settings by visiting this link.
  3. Find the Travel Companion App in the list of active apps and click Remove.
  4. Once you remove the app, a data deletion request will automatically be sent to our system.
  5. If you would like to manually request data deletion, you can contact us via email at Waziullah Kayes.

Additional Information

For any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact our support team at Waziullah Kayes.
